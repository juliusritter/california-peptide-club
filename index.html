About

The California Peptide Club is a private collective of physicians, researchers, entrepreneurs, and biohackers exploring the frontier of peptide science, based out of San Francisco, California. We gather to share protocols, review emerging data, and push the boundaries of human performance and longevity—with the rigor of scholars and the curiosity of gods.

Ethos

“No dogma. Just transparency, real science, and a shared commitment to access and knowledge.”

This is not another panel where sponsors pitch from the stage. We are an industry-facing community built on radical honesty. Every voice at the table earns its place through expertise, not affiliation. We believe peptide science should be accessible, quality-controlled, and driven by people who actually understand synthesis from the root up.

Pillars

Knowledge

Peer-reviewed research. Real-world protocols. Shelf stability studies, HPLC, and supply chain transparency—not hearsay.

Community

A vetted circle of concierge longevity physicians, high-performance founders, and elite athletes. No tourists.

Optimization

Longevity. Recovery. Cognition. Performance. The full spectrum of what peptides can unlock for human potential.

The Landscape

The peptide industry is at an inflection point. Legislative meetings with RFK and key figures in the Trump administration are reshaping the regulatory terrain weekly. Compounding pharmacies, research hubs, and telehealth platforms all operate in shifting gray areas.

Compliance frameworks change by the month. The difference between operating safely and recklessly has never been thinner—or more consequential.

We exist to make sense of the chaos together.

Voices at the Table

The Legal Mind

The Athlete
